Status logic — Cancelled: P.O Remarks = "Cancelled". Delivered On Time: has a Received Date on or before Delivery Date. Delivered But Delayed: Received Date after Delivery Date. Open Orders: no Received Date and not flagged as Cancelled or Undelivered — regardless of whether the Delivery Date has passed.
