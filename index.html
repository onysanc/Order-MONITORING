Status logic — Cancelled: P.O Remarks = "Cancelled". Delivered On Time: has a Received Date on or before Delivery Date. Delivered But Delayed: Received Date after Delivery Date. Undelivered: no Received Date and P.O Remarks = "Undelivered" (explicitly flagged). Open Orders: no Received Date and not flagged as Cancelled or Undelivered — regardless of whether the Delivery Date has passed. Adjust the computeStatus() function in the page source if your business rule differs.
